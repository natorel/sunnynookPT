Frequently Asked Questions

CCPT is most effective for children aged 3 to 10, though the approach can be adapted for children up to 12. If you're unsure whether your child is a good fit, feel free to reach out and we can chat through it.

Sessions are typically 50 minutes. This includes time for the child's play session as well as a brief check-in with parents/carers.

Every child is different. Research suggests that CCPT is most effective when sessions are consistent and ongoing — typically a minimum of 10–15 sessions. We'll review progress together as we go.

For most sessions, children attend the playroom on their own. This is an important part of the CCPT process — it gives children the freedom to lead and express themselves without worry. I'll keep you informed every step of the way.

Please get in touch to discuss rebate options, as these can depend on your circumstances and referral pathway. I'm happy to help you navigate what's available.
