What is Play Therapy?

Play is a child's natural language.
Toys are their words.

Child-Centred Play Therapy (CCPT) is a specialised, evidence-based mental health intervention for children aged 3 to 12. Unlike traditional approaches which may ask children to articulate complex emotions they may not yet have the vocabulary for, CCPT meets them where they are.

In the playroom, I provide a safe, non-judgmental environment where your child leads the way. Guided by the philosophies of Virginia Axline and Carl Rogers, I rely on the child's innate "growth force" — their natural drive to heal and self-actualise.

Who Can It Help?

CCPT is beneficial for children navigating…

🌿

Anxiety & Stress

Helping children process "big worries" and find a sense of inner calm.

💛

Emotional Regulation

Teaching children how to identify, express, and manage intense feelings like anger or sadness.

🤝

Social & Behavioural Challenges

Improving self-control, reducing impulsivity, and enhancing how they relate to peers and family.

🌱

Trauma & Life Transitions

Providing a safe space to process big changes like divorce, grief, or moving to a new country.

⭐

Self-Esteem & Resilience

Building a strong sense of "I can" by allowing the child to lead their own therapeutic journey.
